Issue:
There is a disconnection between the incoming charging port to the battery side, along the PCB.
See images and video for diagnostics.

###

Solution attempts:
The FSH6 mainboard is replaceable from R&S with part number 1157.3570.00 according to the manual.
I have sent part requests to two sites (wbparts.com and www.csgparts.com) without receiving any reply.

The next step might be to register on Rohde & Schwarz site and get in contact with them directly:
https://www.rohde-schwarz.com/us/service-support/customer-self-service-applications/supportcenter_258469.html

Another suggestion is to charge the battery on the side in a specific charging station, and then swap out the batteries. The downside is that the battery swap is quite a hefty operation. See the service manual for further details:
Alltest-Rohde-and-Schwarz-FSH6-ServiceManual.pdf
