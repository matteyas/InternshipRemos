Modbus Device Wiring Overview

1) Connect power to 7~36V and GND
[modbus-power.png]

2) AI1 to AI8 on the Modbus device represents the analog inputs
Default configuration:
Modbus		External
AI1	<->	HPA 1 Voltage Monitor Signal
AI2	<->	HPA 2 Voltage Monitor Signal
[modbus-analog-inputs.png]

3) Connect the RS485 interface
Modbus		Raspberry Pi
A+	<->	A+
B-	<->	B-
[modbus-rs485-001.png]
[modbus-rs485-002.png]
